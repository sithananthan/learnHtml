Aruvi Iyarkai Sandhai

Aruvi iyarkai sandhai is an organic store which is based out of Puducherry. It was started on Jan 2018, and its main moto is to sell organic produce in an affordable price and it should be available to all the people. The products especially fruits and vegetables are locally produced by local farmers. They use no chemical fertilizer in their farming technique and hence all the products we sell are completely natural and organic one.

We Plant our future

Today's children doesnt like to play outside or in sun, like previous generatio did. They have very less knowledge about food they eat, how is it grown and less awareness about environment. This Project is to help kids from urban school to learn about food and agriculture. This would be part of their daily curriculum. So learn Maths and science concepts through agriculture.

Ancient Egypt - THE Pharoah

The Pharoah was a living god. He was at the top of Ancient Egyptian society. The pharoah was responsible for the well being of his people and was obligated to keep the order. He was all powerful and he spent most of his time at the capital city of Memphis. Here he administered control of both Lower and Upper Egypt.

The Tombs of the Pharoahs

The Pharoah spent most of his life working on his tomb. The Pharoah's had this busy afterlife where they continued to control the universe after their death. The god Anubis assisted the pharoah in the mummfication process. After the pharoah died he traveled to the underworld using the Book of the Dead.
