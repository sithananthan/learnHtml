    Aruvi's first farm tour was organized last week for the customers. We were taking them to the farm where we procure our few of our products. One of visitors, who had came for farm tour is a third year agriculture student. She loved visiting the farm and she had lot of questions to the farmer since there is lot of difference between how healthy good food is grown and how it is taught in the college. she also want her friends to visit this farm. Special thanks to Farmer who made special breakfast for the visitors thinai kanchi, freshly harvested watermelon and Salted mango was a great combination of food to have in a farm. We loved the plate on which our healthy food was served for us. thanks to maduraikavi Ayya once again
